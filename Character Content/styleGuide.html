Style Guide:

Color pallet:

The reason why i chose the pallet included in the style guide is because, i wanted a very minimalistic look. Mosty due to the fact that Xolani is a very modest and humble person. So, i thought the colors included are indicative of his personality type.

Fonts Used:

For the styling of the text. I wanted something that is very readable so, sticking to font that is from the san-serif familiy was very important to me. Because i love their legibility. So since much of the design was user-centric the aim was to fascilitate an experience that doesint come with any hassels.

Overal Styling:

And lastly for the styling of navigation, the aim was also to be minimalistic, so that the functions of the site are easily found or readable. The Navigation links have two states, one indicative of a user hovering on the navigation ui and the second, when the user is not hovering on the button, which is left as plain text. The reason for changing colour when hovering is because i wanted there to be some level of feedback to indicate whether a button is pressed hopefully such interactions will also be implemented across the site through the development of the website.
