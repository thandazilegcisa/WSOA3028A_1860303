About:

Hi, my name is Xolani i like to refer to myself as a Zeitgeist, which means "Spirit of the age". I am a cultural historian who lives in Johannesburg.Though i grew up in Gqeberha, in the Eastern Cape My particular areas of interest are art, games, and music. I am dedicated both to research and practice of cultural history or using cultural artefact and history into creating and understanding the world we live in.

The ultimate goal of my research is to see and understand Johannesburg from the lens of its history and culture. More specifically music. The aim is to illustrate how Sophiatown as a space, cultivated cultural artefacts. And what meanings did residents reflect in these artefact. I also consider how the meanings inscribed in the art created in Sophiatown reflect the time and space within which the artists operated and how their operation was organised.

Therefore, the reason why i chose and created this character is because i wanted to use the internet as messanger that archives organises history. Hence, this web pages aims to democratise information and become an extension of the interent in which, the topics that i or others would wish to talk about are represented in what i am offering. Despite the fact that this knowledge has been accounted for and the fact that i can execute the project is testament to that, i wanted to focus on the story of the subject in question from the basis of the cultural artefacts that it has created in as a result of histroy.
