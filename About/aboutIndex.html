About:

Hi, my name is Xolani i like to refer to myself as a Zeitgeist, which means "Spirit of the age". I am a 20 year old skateboarder who lives in Johannesburg. I am mostly a street skateboarder, although i first started out as a cruising skateboarder, skating just for the sake of travelling to and from school. I then started learning tricks as i continued to travel around Johannesburg going to different parks, making new friends and ultimately learnt street and park skateboarding tricks. The first trick i ever learnt, though admittedly after several bails, "credit cards", and knee scrapes was a kick flip. It was a very proud moment, that i will never forget and whenever i am not fliping tricks, i am cruising taking photographs of beautiful metropolitan Johannesburg. This Character is loosely based off of a friend of mine that i have not seen in a very long while. So i guess this website is an attempt to render him visible and somewhat accessibile because "The medium is the message".
