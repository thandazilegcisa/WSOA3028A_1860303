How does the Medium is the Message impact our understanding of the web

Thandazile Gcisa
1 April 2021

In the book: The medium is the message Marshal McLuhan writes that

"The medium is the message because it is the medium that shapes and controls the scale and form of human association and action and thus the medium in which information is communicated through is more important than the message itself"
(McLuhan, 1964)

To understand what this means we have to understand what medium and message mean. Medium, refers to the tools or instruments used to provide agency to a user. Message refers to the contextual underpinnings that tools use to cultivate meaning through the use of semiotics such as text, images, and audio all of which are hypermedia which provide an extension outside the traditional analogue media constraints.

With this knowledge we are able to understand the world wide web as a mechanic that functions as an interconnected continuum of inter-textual meanings created by the ability for the medium to have an adverse effect on the scale and form of human action. Therefore,through the structure and configuration of hypermedia, and hyperlinking. It becomes clear that the non-linear reception and reciprocation of threads of information between a network of people sharing and retrieving information on a distributed network is the message.
